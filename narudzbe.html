Transfeero Fidelity Program: ONEMILE - Terms and Conditions

Welcome to the ONEMILE fidelity program, brought to you exclusively by Transfeero! This innovative program is meticulously designed to reward our loysal customers and make your journey with us even more rewarding. Here's everything you need to know about ONEMILE and how you can make the most of its fantastic benefits.

Program Levels and Discounts:
ONEMILE offers four exciting levels of membership, each with its own set of perks. As you accumulate miles with our service, you ascend to higher levels which unlock greater discounts:
- Level 1: Upon reaching 50 miles, members will enjoy a 5% discount on all bookings.
- Level 2: At 300 miles, the discount increases to 8% on all bookings.
- Level 3: Achieving 600 miles secures a generous 10% discount on all bookings.

Its important to highlight that each destination with Transfeero comes with a minimum booking amount, which is non-discountable. This ensures that the quality and reliability of our services remain uncompromised.

Program Duration:
The ONEMILE program commences on the 1st of January each year and concludes on the 31st of December, unless a new communication is issued.

Membership Subscription:
Joining the ONEMILE program is absolutely free and the subscription will remain so indefinitely. There's no catch - just sign up and start accruing your miles towards fantastic discounts.

Mileage Retention and Loss:
If you reach at least the first level (50 miles) by the end of the program year, your status will be carried over into the new program year. However, if you have not reached the first level, the accumulated miles will expire and cannot be rolled over.

Yearly Renewal:
While we strive to continuously offer the ONEMILE program, its annual renewal is not guaranteed, and we reserve the right to adjust or discontinue the program based on operational requirements.

Miles Accumulation:
Earn miles with each ride that is successfully completed through Transfeero. It is important to note that miles are only credited after the journey has been fulfilled. In the case of a ride that is cancelled or refunded, any previously earned miles from that ride will be reversed.

Mileage Counting:
Miles will be credited to your account within a few days after the ride has been completed. In the event that you notice a discrepancy or missing miles for a completed ride, please reach out to us for assistance.

Marketing Communications:
By participating in the ONEMILE program, you agree to receive promotional emails from Transfeero regarding the program and related offers.

We trust that the ONEMILE program will enhance your travel experience and offer you value as a token of our appreciation for your continued support. Start earning your miles today, and watch your discounts take off with every Transfeero ride you book.

For more information or to enroll in the ONEMILE fidelity program, visit our website today at www.transfeero.com. Let your travels be miles ahead with ONEMILE.
